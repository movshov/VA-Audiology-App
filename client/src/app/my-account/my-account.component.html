Password Requirements

A password must be at least 8 characters long, six of the characters cannot occur more than once, and it must include characters from at least 3 of the following categories:

  • Lowercase English characters: a-z
  • Uppercase English characters: A-Z
  • Special characters: !@#$%^&*()-+
  • Digits: 0-9

Consider using a passphrase as your password, they are much easier to remember, but much harder for a computer to guess.

A passphrase is a series of unrelated words or a sentence (not a popular quote or song), such as:

Battery-sunset ate 3 OR I bought 12 donuts!

Maximum Password length is 72 characters.
